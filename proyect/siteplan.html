Site Plan: Custom 3D Printing

Site Name

Name: 3D Printing Bolivia

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates that the service focuses on custom 3D printing within Bolivia and is easy to remember.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to offer personalized 3D printing services. Users will be able to:

  • View ready-to-print products or purchase them directly.
  • Request quotes for custom pieces by uploading their 3D files (such as .stl).
  • Contact the company directly through a contact form.
  • Learn more about the materials, printing processes, and company values.

Scenarios

  • How can I upload my custom design to request a quote?
  • What materials do you use for printing the products?
  • Where can I see examples of previous projects?
  • How do I contact the company in case of an urgent request?

Color Schema

Selected site colors:

  • Blue (#3498db): Main color for headings, buttons, and navigation elements.
  • Orange (#e67e22): Accent color for secondary buttons and calls to action.
